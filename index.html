ABOUT PUBG

  • Massive Maps: Explore huge and diverse battlegrounds filled with cities, forests, deserts, and military zones. Every map offers unique terrain, hidden loot spots, and endless tactical possibilities that make each match feel different.
  • Battle Royale: Fight against players from around the world in intense last-man-standing combat. Drop from the sky, collect weapons and gear, survive the shrinking zone, and battle your way to victory.
  • Constant Updates: Experience new maps, weapons, game modes, events, and gameplay improvements through regular updates that keep the action fresh and exciting for every player.
  • Global Community: Join millions of players worldwide, team up with friends, compete with rivals, and become part of a massive global gaming community built around teamwork and competition.
  • Ranked Play: Test your skills in competitive ranked matches, climb the leaderboards, earn exclusive rewards, and prove you have what it takes to become a true PUBG champion.
